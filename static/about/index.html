About

Thirteenth Parallel was an early attempt at a DHTML e-zine, covering technology, patterns, and ideas pertaining to the cutting edge of web development. The real world caught up with us and we didn't have enough time to maintain the project, but the ideas are still as relevent in today's web2.0 world as they were back before the term ajax had been coined. This site will remain as a minimal archive.
